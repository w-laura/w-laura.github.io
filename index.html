About me

Hello, I'm Laura. As a student specializing in computer graphics, computer vision, and machine learning, I am deeply passionate about the movie industry, especially animation and special effects. I am utterly fascinated by how technology can be leveraged to create stunning visual effects that bring imaginary worlds to life. I am a big fan of movies and enjoy studying and analyzing the technical aspects of how they are made. In addition to my technical pursuits, I also have a strong interest in art, particularly sketching and painting. I am convinced that my artistic background gives me a unique perspective when it comes to developing new and innovative visual effects. My ultimate goal is to become a skilled computer scientist who can contribute to the industry by using my expertise to create even more amazing and breathtaking visual effects. I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and am enthusiastic about the potential for technology and art to transform the movie industry.

HoloHue

HoloHue is a mixed reality application for controlling smart lights using the Microsoft HoloLens 2. The project was part of the Mixed Reality lecture at ETH Zurich in 2022.

Computer Graphics - Rendering Competition

As part of the Computer Graphics course at ETH Zurich 2022 we implemented a renderer based on Nori, that allows us to create realistic images using ray-tracing. At the end of the semester I was able to qualify for the rendering competition with my final rendering with the theme "out of place".
